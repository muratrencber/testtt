1 Introduction

1.1 Scope

CarShare360 is a platform enabling car owners to share their vehicles conveniently. Our service focuses on geolocation-based vehicle sharing via a user-friendly app. Car owners can easily list their cars for short-term rentals, fostering a collaborative and sustainable transportation solution. Importantly, CarShare360 excludes payment services, ensuring a straightforward and secure vehicle-sharing experience.

1.2 Deliverables

  1. Backend Development
  2. Serverless Design & Development
  3. Product Development & Admin Panel
  4. UI Design & Implementation
  5. Mobile Development

1.4 Non-Functional Issues

1.4.1 Performance

High performance is a critical aspect of CarShare360, ensuring a swift and responsive experience for both car owners and renters. It influences the speed at which users can locate available cars, make bookings, and update their profiles. Eﬀicient serverless architecture, database optimization, and load testing will be employed to guarantee that our app can handle a significant number of simultaneous users without any delays. A responsive and speedy platform enhances user satisfaction and encourages them to engage with the service more frequently.

1.4.2 Usability

Usability is pivotal in making CarShare360 an accessible and user-friendly platform, tailored for Android and iOS. The design and navigation must be intuitive, simplifying tasks for car owners when listing their vehicles and aiding renters in locating and booking cars effortlessly. A user-friendly interface will be a key driver of user adoption and retention.

1.4.3 Cross-Platform Compatibility

While CarShare360 is designed for mobile devices on Android and iOS, cross-platform compatibility remains a priority. It means that the app will provide a consistent and efficient experience across different smartphones and tablets. By optimizing for different mobile operating systems, including iOS and Android, we ensure that all users enjoy a seamless and user-friendly experience, leading to increased engagement and broader adoption.
